IxD PROCESS

Goal Alignment

As discussed in my last blog post, my goal is to create a balance between readability, creativity and aesthetic appeal to provide an engaging user experience. I want to prioritize legible typography, well-structured content and intuitive navigation. The design will incorporate unique and engaging elements. To bring across the aesthetic appeal, I will focus on cohesive colours and balanced layouts, allowing for users to not be overwhelmed with information and create a memorable experience.

Focusing on my goal alignment and the structure of my website, I want it to be presented professionally and attract the right audiences. My target audience for potential employers or clients. I want people in the animation, game design and web development industry to view my website as a portfolio of my work. The website will be a portfolio showcasing: animations, games, my process of developing a website, logos and information about me.
